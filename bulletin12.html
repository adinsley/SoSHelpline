Spring Book Sale

Don't forget - the SOS Help Spring Book Sale is this weekend! Mark your calendars for our spring book sale and come see us at our new location:

March 20, 2010
12:00 pm to 4:00 pm
Orrick Law Offices
31, avenue Pierre 1er de Serbie, 75016, Paris


Here is some additional info about the sale:

Books
  • Paperbacks: 1€
  • Hard covers: 2€
  • Tons of books in every category

    • Set of espresso cups, courtesy of Smash Porcelaine, value 15€

    • 2 takeouts for 2, courtesy of Chal India, value 25€

And don't forget to tell your friends about this great way to get inexpensive books and snacks, meet other people in the English-speaking community, and support our organisation!
