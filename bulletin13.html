A Big Thank You

Thanks to everyone who made the SOS Help Spring Book Sale a success! Because of your support, we raised 4350€ for SOS Help.

We'd like to give a special thanks to the following:
  • Everyone who bought books, tea corner goodies, and raffle tickets
  • Everyone who donated books
  • Orrick, for lending us the new premises
  • WH Smith, for donating books for the raffle
  • Smash Porcelaine, for donating hand-made espresso cups for the raffle
  • Indian Ocean, for donating an "Initiation to Bollywood" dance class for the raffle
  • Chal India, for donating a 1-hour cooking class and 2 takeaway dinners for the raffle
  • Restaurant Karaikal, for donating a dinner for two for the raffle
  • Sugarplum Cake Shop, for donating goodies to the tea corner
  • FunnySpoon, for donating goodies to the tea corner

All proceeds benefit SOS Help, with our two book sales per year accounting for a large percentage of our fundraising efforts. We greatly appreciate your support!

If you missed this sale, be sure to come to our next one! And if you came to this sale, please be sure to spread the word to your friends about this great way to get inexpensive books and help SOS Help at the same time!
