October 10, 2009

October is busy at SOS Help!

The book sale is only two weeks away and our Writers at the Residence fundraiser is only 10 days away - just in time to get your tickets! Check out the details below for all that's going on with SOS Help this October.

Stock up on English books

The book sale is in just two weeks! As usual, hard covers are 2€, paperbacks are 1€, and we'll have plenty of tasty treats at our tea corner for a quick refreshment during your shopping.

When and where:
   Saturday, October 24th
   12pm to 4pm
   St. Joseph's Church
   50, Avenue Hoche, 75008 Paris

Wanna help? You can:
   1. Bring books the day of the sale
   2. Help set up Friday (10/23) anytime from 2-6 pm
   3. Help break down on Saturday anytime from 4-6 pm

Want more info? Check out our website.

Meet English-Speaking Authors at the British Embassy

Join us for a fun evening with English-speaking authors, great food and drinks, and the unique atmosphere of the Residence at the British Embassy.

The event features Stephen Clarke, Bryce Corbett, Alec Lobrano, Michael Sadler, Heather Stimmler-Hall, and Charles Timoney.

When and where:
   October 20th
   6:30 pm
   British Embassy Residence
   39 rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, 75008 Paris

The event is a fundraiser for SOS Help so please join us and encourage your friends to do so as well! Tickets are 45€ (+ 2€ per order if booked via PayPal).

Tickets must be ordered by October 15th and there are only 50 left so book today! (For bookings after the 15th, please call 06 11 71 32 03 to check for availability)
