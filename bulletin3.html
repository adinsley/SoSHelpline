Feeling Down? Call us up!

If you or someone you know is feeling lonely, sad, or depressed, give us a call!

We're here to take your call every day from 3 pm to 11 pm on 01 46 21 46 46.

Pass it on

Happy New Year!

Happy New Year from all of us at SOS Help. As we go into our 35th year of providing our listening service to English speakers in France, we want to thank everyone who continues to support our organization and make our services possible.

Thanks for another great year and we hope 2009 will be just as successful!

Volunteer with SOS Help

SOS Help is always looking for new volunteers to help by either listening on the hotline or supporting the listeners through publicity and fundraising efforts.

Our next recruitment dates are January 29th, February 4th, and February 10th at 7pm at the American Church of Paris, 65 Quai d'Orsay, 75007, Paris. Please note: you only have to attend ONE session!

Check out our website for more information on SOS Help and what we're looking for in a volunteer.

Spring Book Sale

Our Spring Book Sale is fast approaching. Stay tuned for an email within the next month announcing the exact date (or bookmark our events page and check back frequently).

In the meantime, we'd greatly appreciate the donation of any books you're finished reading. You may bring your books the day of the sale or for donations of 100+ books, you may call us on 01 46 21 46 46 to arrange a pickup.

Donations Welcome

We are dependent upon the support and generosity of the English-speaking community in France to stay up and running. In addition to supporting our twice-annual book sales, we are grateful for the donations made by cash and cheque throughout the year. Thanks to everyone who donated in 2008!

If you'd like to support us with a donation for 2009, you may send a cheque to:

SOS Help
BP 43
Boulogne Cedex 92101

Please note that 66% of your donations are tax-deductible. A tax receipt is available upon request. Additionally, a copy of our annual report is available upon request.
