"Bag of Books" Sale

Stock up on summer reading for 5€ per bag!

Yep, you heard right. Following the success of last year's everything-must-go sale, we're having another one! Fill a bag as full as you can for only 5€ - what a deal! So swing by St. Joe's (we'll be set up outside on the steps) and score some new summer reading while supporting a great cause!

Saturday, June 25th
1 pm to 3 pm
