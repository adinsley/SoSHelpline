March 30, 2009

A Record-Breaking Success!

A big thank you to everyone who bought books at our sale as well as donated books. We just had the best sale yet - the proceeds from the books and tea corner were over 5400 euros!

We'd also like to thank Europcar who generously donated the use of a moving van to transport the books to and from our storage unit.

Thanks again, and see you at our autumn sale!

All proceeds benefit SOS Help. Visit our website for future events and more info about SOS Help.
