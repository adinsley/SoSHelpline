skype us!

SOS Help is currently trialling a free online English language listening service using the popular Internet voice service "Skype".

You can call our experienced listeners via the Skype ID "callsoshelp" or just click on the link below to connect directly with us during our hours of operation.

The service is available between 15:00 and 23:00 (Paris time, GMT+1).

There is no charge to you for this call. As always, your privacy is our primary concern. This means that the listener taking your Skype call will not be able to see any of your Skype details and your confidentiality is assured.

In order to protect your anonymity and ours we do not offer video calls.

For more on how to install and configure Skype please visit Skype.

It's currently in Paris.
