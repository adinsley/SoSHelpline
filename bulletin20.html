A Season of Giving

While the holidays are a merry time of year for most, they can be hard for people who are far from family and friends, grieving a loved one, or simply feeling lonely.

Thanks to your support and the efforts of our volunteers, SOS Help is there for them, not just during the holiday season, but every day of the year for the past 36 years.

We rely on your generous donations to keep the line open. Any amount is greatly appreciated! Below are examples of how the funds help our organisation:

  • 20€ prints 100 brochures
  • 60€ covers phone and internet for 1 month
  • 135€ pays for 1 listener training session
  • 200€ covers rent for 1 month

You can donate securely via PayPal on our website or you can mail a check (details in the column to the right).
