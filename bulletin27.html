Send Printed Cards and eCards to Friends and Family Back Home

SOS Help is funded fully through donations from generous people in the community, both English and French-speaking. Our newest support comes from Kisseo and ooprint, who are both part of Groupe Aventers. We appreciate their generosity! These two businesses offer services that are of particular interest for English speakers living in Paris:

Kisseo offers unique eCards for every occasion, such as birthdays, love, thank you, and Christmas. This is great for expats abroad since you can easily stay connected with friends & family back home. Staying in touch can ease the transition into your new life in France, which is obviously important to us at SOS Help!

ooprint online printing allows you to choose and design your printed products online with your own text, photos, images, and logo, then they deliver it anywhere in the world! For expats, this is a particularly great way to get holiday greeting cards, invitations, business cards, and other printed materials in English when in France.

Thanks again to both companies for their support of our organization. If you know of a company that would like to support us (and increase awareness with our audience at the same time!), please contact admin@soshelpline.org.
