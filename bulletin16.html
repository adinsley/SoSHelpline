The SOS Help Bulletin

July 5, 2010

A Big Thank You

Thanks to everyone who made the SOS Help Summer Clearance Book Sale a success! Because of your support, we raised 1,050€ for SOS Help.

We'd like to give a special thanks to the following:
  • Everyone who bought books
  • Everyone who helped out
  • St. Joseph's for having us back at their premises
  • All the bloggers and webmasters who helped get the word out about the event
  • AngloInfo and AAWE for promoting us on their sites

All proceeds benefit SOS Help, with our book sales accounting for a large percentage of our fundraising efforts. We greatly appreciate your support!

If you missed this sale, be sure to come to our next one on October 10 at Orrick Law Offices (details to be announced soon)! And if you came to this sale, please be sure to spread the word to your friends about this great way to get inexpensive books and help SOS Help at the same time!
