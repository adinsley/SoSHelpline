October 26, 2009

SOS Help is on a roll

October has been a busy month for us - first with the "Writers at the Residence" fundraiser hosted by Lady Westmacott at the Residence at the British Embassy, then with our Autumn Book Sale at St. Joseph's Church. We brought in almost €8,000 at the "Writers" event and €5,480 from the book sale.

So we'd like to say a big thank you to everyone who attended the events and/or donated.

Lady Westmacott introducing the panel.

Visit our website to see the photos of both events and be sure to read what Heather Stimmler-Hall wrote about us on her blog.

Thanks again, and see you at our spring sale!

All proceeds benefit SOS Help. Visit our website for more info about SOS Help.

It's not too late to donate!

SOS Help relies on donations and fundraisers to keep our line open year-round. Your donation is 66% tax-deductible under French law. Visit our website for more information on how to donate.

We really appreciate your support!
