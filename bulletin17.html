Autumn Book Sale

We're excited to announce that the SOS Help Autumn Book Sale will be held again at Orrick. Shoppers at the spring sale enjoyed the new location so if you haven't been yet be sure to stop by and browse for books in a space that's twice as large! Also, please note we've switched the sale to a Sunday.

October 10, 2010
12:00 pm to 4:00 pm
Orrick Law Offices
31, avenue Pierre 1er de Serbie, 75016, Paris

As usual, hard covers will be 2 euros and paperbacks will be 1 euro. All proceeds benefit SOS Help. Visit our website for more info.
