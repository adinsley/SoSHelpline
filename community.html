additional emotional support lines

  • SOS Amitié, 09 72 39 40 50, a French-speaking line
  • Suicide Ecoute, 01 45 39 40 00, a French-speaking line
  • The Samaritans, (44) (0)8457 90 90 90, a line based in the UK
  • Befrienders International, listeners in other countries
  • suicide.org
  • IASP

mental health resources

  • Counselling in France, lists counselling professionals by French region and departments, and information on various UK based helplines
  • AngloINFO (Paris), listings are available in the directories on the various AngloINFO regional sites
  • Paris Therapy Service, list of English speaking mental health professionals in the Paris area
  • ICS International Counseling Service, Paris area, 01 45 50 26 49

other english speaking support services in france

  • Alcoholics Anonymous, meetings in France
  • American Aid Society, 07 69 74 31 97, assistance and advice for Americans in need in France
  • Cancer Support France, 0800 240 200
  • British Charitable Fund, 01 47 59 07 69, 10:00 to 17:00 Monday - Thursday, a UK registered charity founded to relieve financial hardship in the British community in France
  • Domestic Violence Help Paris, Free support group
  • Espace Famille, Paris, family mediation in English, fees are income related
  • Overeaters Anonymous, Wellness Support Group: dealing with depression. Peer group for those diagnosed with anxiety, depression or bi-polar disorder. Meetings at America Cathedral Paris 2x monthly judy4today@gmail.com

elsewhere

  • Pathways to Safety International, 0800 99 0011 then 866 879 6636, toll-free, Monday - Friday 24 hrs, or email crisis@pathwaystosafety.org

anglophone organisations

  • AAWE Association of American Women in Europe
  • Association of American Residents Overseas, a volunteer, non-partisan organization representing the interests of Americans living and working abroad
  • British and Commonwealth Women's Association, organises social and other events for women who are, or who are married to, British and Commonwealth nationals
  • British Community Committee, coordinates the activities of British social, educational, and other organisations in the Paris region
  • Council for the English-Speaking Community (CESC), comprehensive listing of English- speaking social, educational, religious and other organisations around Paris
  • MESSAGE Mother Support Group, provides a support network for English-speaking parents or parents-to-be in the Paris area
  • SPRINT, a non-profit organization group of English-speaking and bilingual professionals who work with children of all ages with special needs. (SPAN is the parent action group who work with SPRINT.)
  • Le WIC de Paris, association of international women
  • WICE, a non-profit cultural and educational association in Paris dedicated to providing courses as well as a broad range of services and resources
