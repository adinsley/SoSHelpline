Spring Book Sale

We're excited to announce that the SOS Help Spring Book Sale will be held again at Orrick. Shoppers at the last sale enjoyed the new location so if you haven't been yet be sure to stop by and browse for books in a space that's twice as large!

As usual, hard covers will be 2 euros and paperbacks will be 1 euro. Also, please note the sale is now on a Sunday. All proceeds benefit SOS Help. Visit our website for more info.

March 20, 2011
12:00 pm to 4:00 pm
Orrick Law Offices
31, avenue Pierre 1er de Serbie, 75016, Paris
