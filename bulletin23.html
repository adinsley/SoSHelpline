Thanks For Your Support!

Our spring book sale and Goodies-To-Go were a huge success! We raised nearly 4,400€, all of which goes to support and run SOS Help 365 days per year.

  • All the volunteers who donated their time and energy to help make this sale a success.
  • Orrick, for lending us their premises.
