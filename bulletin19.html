Thanks For Your Support!

Our fall book and bake sale was another great success! We raised nearly 3600€, all of which goes to support and run SOS Help.

We'd like to give a special thanks to the following:

  • Everyone who bought books and snacks. We appreciate you taking time out of your busy weekend to support our cause!
  • Everyone who donated books and goodies, including Sugarplum Cake Shop, FunnySpoon, and Chal India.
  • All the volunteers who donated their time and energy to help make this sale a success.
  • Orrick, for lending us their premises.

See you at our spring sale!
